Skills

About me

I am a software developer based in Bristol. I strive to deliver only the highest quality product to end users and take great pride in doing so.

With my skillset, I can overcome a multitude of software development challenges. That said, I am also highly-motivated to constantly learn new languages, technologies and methodologies to take on new challenges and believe this is one of the most exciting aspects of the job.

On this website, you can find my work experience and relevant qualifications, as well as examples of work that demonstrate my ability to plan, propose, develop and test a software project from start to finish by myself.

Services

Some of the services I offer in my line of work

Design Wireframing

Before starting any new app or web development, I ensure that a thorough wireframe and functionality plan is in place. I use specialised software, such as Figma, or hand and paper for this.

Responsive Design

I am keenly aware of the importance of responsive design in app and web development. To that end, I am experienced at using frameworks like Bootstrap or media queries for mobile-first development.

Frontend Development

I am experienced at developing app and web frontends with a variety of different tech stacks (see my skills section above to get an idea of what I can do). I always develop frontends with UX in mind.

Backend Development

I am experienced at developing backends with PHP or Express.js. In my projects section below, you can find examples of connecting frontend with backend to make API calls or database queries.

Database Management

Database management is a crucial skill for almost everyone working in software development. I am experienced at managing MySQL, PostgreSQL and NoSQL databases.

Hardware Application

I have been building my own PCs for different operating systems for years and enjoy working on software with real-world interactivity, such as phone apps or Raspberry Pi/Arduino apps.

Education

Freelance Developer

2021 - 2024

I worked as a part-time Freelance Developer whilst working as a Barista and later a Labourer.

I completed web development work for clients whilst I built my portfolio e.g. Kelly's Kollars, a website for a dog-walking company. I also worked on Bluetooth/Wi-Fi integration with Arduino/ESP32-based robots to gain some embedded development experience.

Full-Stack Developer Trainee

2023-2024

Having a few years of self-taught coding experience already and desiring a full-time career as a software developer, I decided to undertake the IT Career Switch Coding Traineeship, which I completed in my spare time over 1 year.

I developed full-stack portfolio projects as part of the traineeship e.g. Gazetteer, Company Directory (details on GitHub / website). I also completed various full-stack training courses as part of the traineeship that have been foundational to my career.

Frontend Developer

2024-present

My primary role is to develop web games with input from adjacent teams e.g. design, art, math, backend, QA, compliance, marketing. My secondary role is to develop the proprietary framework / tooling that our studio uses to develop web games.
